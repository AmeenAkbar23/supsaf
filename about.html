The Purpose of supsaf

supsaf is an open-source platform designed for the pure appreciation of photography. Our mission is to provide a dedicated space for high-resolution images of places, nature, and objects, strictly excluding human subjects to maintain a focus on the environment and design.

Non-Human Focus

We believe beauty exists in the world around us. We focus on landscapes, architecture, and objects.

High Resolution

Every image on supsaf is high-quality and searchable via a modern keyword API.

Simple Interaction

Enjoy content without the noise. We support likes and shares, but keep the site clean by removing comments.

Open Source

Built as a community-driven project.
